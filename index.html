.About Me

After working as a teacher for over a dozen years in Baltimore, I moved back to my hometown to be a full-time mom to my two adorable and energetic kiddos. I was enjoying this new role but felt the creative energy building up inside. I’ve always loved drawing, design, painting and making things with my hands. I started making decorative cakes on a whim to unleash some of that creative energy. I wanted a challenge for myself and cake making has provided that and a lot more.

I love learning new skills and planning each cake I create. It’s brought me into contact with new people and the entire process makes me happy! I’m looking forward to learning new skills and trying out the sweet Russian piping tips I received for Christmas. A special thanks to my mom and dad for helping with the kiddos when I’m elbows deep in powdered sugar!
